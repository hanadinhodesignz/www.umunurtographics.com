About Us

The Somali Community Solidarity Centre (SCSC) is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting Somali communities through education, advocacy, and social services. We promote inclusion, equity, and empowerment for Somali individuals and families worldwide.

Our Mission

  • Promote cultural understanding and unity within Somali communities.
  • Support youth and families through educational programs and mentorship.
  • Advocate for social justice, equality, and access to resources.
  • Encourage leadership and community participation at all levels.
