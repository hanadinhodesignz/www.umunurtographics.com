About Us

The Somali Community Solidarity Centre (SCSC) is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting Somali communities through education, advocacy, and social services. We strive to promote inclusivity, equity, and opportunity for all.

Our Mission

  • Foster cultural understanding and social inclusion.
  • Support youth and families with educational programs.
  • Advocate for equal rights and social justice.
  • Build a strong and resilient Somali community.
